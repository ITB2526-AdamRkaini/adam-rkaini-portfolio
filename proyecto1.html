Radio Frequency Exploration and Homemade Receivers

This is a comprehensive research project (Treball de Recerca) that investigates the evolution of radio as a mass communication tool. It includes a deep dive into the physics of the electromagnetic spectrum and a practical experimental phase focused on capturing different radio frequencies using a custom-built homemade receiver.
